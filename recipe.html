Fluffy Pancakes

Delicious and easy-to-make pancakes, perfect for breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it with butter.
  5. Pour small portions of the batter into the pan to form pancakes.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve with honey, fruits, or your favorite toppings!
